What is the STM32 Step-Up Board and Why buy it?

8-bit microcontrollers are the stepping stones for any electronics/embedded engineer or maker, but we all know that these are not sufficient. Industry-recognized microcontrollers are based on ARM Cortex architecture. For most people, shifting to these microcontrollers is either too difficult or the path is confusing/unclear. The STM32 Step-Up Board solves these problems by making everything easier for any beginner to transition to industry-recognized microcontrollers. The STM32 Step-Up board is an STM32F103C8T6 32-bit ARM microcontroller-based development board designed with beginners in mind. This will be achieved by:
1. Bridging the design flaws you will find in many STM32 boards.
2. Preloading the bootloader so you do not have to face the hassle of programmers and flashing bootloaders. You can directly program the controller and get started with your engineering journey.
3. Very shortly, ElectronHub will release getting started written and visual tutorials for the board, which will cover common topics so that you can kick-start your journey with the controller board with ease. Arduino IDE and STM CubeIDE will be used for the same."
